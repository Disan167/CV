Help & Support

How do I add an expense?

To add an expense, navigate to the 'Add Expense' page, fill in the required fields like amount, category, and description, then click 'Save'.

How do I set a budget?

You can set a budget by navigating to the 'Budget' page, then entering a category and amount in the form. Once submitted, your budget will be saved.

How do I view my reports?

Your reports can be viewed under the 'Reports' section. It shows a summary of your expenses, categories, and budget performance over time.
